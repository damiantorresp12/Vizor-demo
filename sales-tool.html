The product

Everything a pre-sale
needs to close.

Six features integrated into a single experience. No apps, no installation, no friction.

01

Rotating aerial renders

Intro with day / dusk / night aerials cycling every 8 seconds. A first impression that sticks.

02

Clickable floor plan polygons

Each unit has a polygon overlay on the plan. Click = full detail panel with price, area, status and gallery.

03

360° tour per room

Navigation hotspots between rooms. Orientation minimap. Built on Pannellum, works offline.

04

Favorites without registration

Clients save units from their phone. No account, no form. Share via WhatsApp in one tap.

05

Native ES / EN bilingual

Full interface in Spanish and English. Hotspots, labels, menus and unit sheets — all translated from day one.

06

Location map with POIs

Leaflet-powered map with curated points of interest. Precise coordinates, clean design, no visual noise.
