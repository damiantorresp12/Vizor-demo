The Workflow

FROM BRIEF
TO FINAL RENDER.

Four steps that take a project from idea to delivery — structured, transparent and collaborative from end to end.

01
Input · You
Initial Brief
Send us a completed brief along with the materials we need to estimate the project and get started — plans, references, 3D models and any information about the look and feel you're after. The clearer the input, the sharper the result.
You provide Plans · references · 3D models · project brief
02
Production · Vizor
3D Process
Our team crafts detailed models that bring your architecture to life. Modeling, materials, lighting and art direction come together to build the scene — every frame produced by hand by our artists: AI-enhanced, never AI-generated.
We craft Modeling · materials · lighting · art direction
03
Collaboration · Together
Stay in Touch
Fluid, constant interaction through the Miro platform in real time. You follow progress, leave comments and request changes at every stage — no long waits, no guessing. Revisions are part of the process, not an afterthought.
In real time Miro board · live feedback · revisions included
04
Delivery · You
Approve & Deliver
Approve the draft version you want us to deliver and we finalize it in full resolution. You receive the final material ready to present, sell and launch — stills, animations, VR tours or a complete interactive sales experience.
You receive Final renders · animations · VR · sales tools
The Pricing Model

ONE BALANCE,
ANY DELIVERABLE.

The same process is powered by VZ Credits — a flexible balance you distribute freely across the deliverables each project needs.

01
Choose a plan
Pick a credit balance — Starter, Pro or Partner. Each plan is a pool of VZ Credits with its own validity period.
02
Allocate your credits
Spend them however your project needs — still images, animations, aerial views or VR tours. No fixed packages.
03
We produce & deliver
Every deliverable runs through the same four-step process above — brief, 3D, real-time review and approval.
